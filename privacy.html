Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

What we collect

Nothing. PowerDoze runs entirely on your local machine. No telemetry, no analytics, no crash reports are sent to any server.

Local data

PowerDoze stores configuration files (rules, power modes, whitelist, analytics) as JSON files in the application directory and %LOCALAPPDATA%\PowerDoze\. These files never leave your computer.

Network access

  • Internal communication: The app's UI talks to its local engine process on your machine. This traffic never reaches the internet.
  • Update check: On startup, the app fetches version.json from our website to check for updates. Only the HTTP request is made; no user data is sent.
  • License activation (Pro): When activating a Pro license, your license key is sent to our server for validation. No personal data beyond the key is transmitted.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties. Payment processing is handled by our payment provider (ECPay / PayPal), which has its own privacy policy.

Your rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. If you uninstall PowerDoze, local data is cleaned up automatically by the uninstaller.

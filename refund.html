Refund Policy

Last updated · 2026-04-27

Our policy

PowerDoze offers a 14-day, no-questions-asked refund for any Pro purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll refund your payment in full.

How to request

Email PowerDozeSupport@gmail.com with your order ID and the email address used for purchase. We'll process your refund within a few business days.

After the refund

Once the refund is processed, your Pro license will be deactivated. You can continue using PowerDoze Free for as long as you like.

How It Works

Three steps to smarter power management.

1

Set Your Rules

Define when your PC should sleep, which apps prevent sleep, and what power mode to use for each time slot.

2

PowerDoze Watches

It monitors the time, running apps, fullscreen state, Wi-Fi network, and your activity level in the background.

3

Auto-Adjusts

Power settings change automatically. No manual switching. Your PC is always in the right mode for what you're doing.

What PowerDoze Does

Windows built-in power plans are static. PowerDoze makes them context-aware.

Scheduled Sleep Rules

Set different sleep timeouts for morning, afternoon, and late night. Filter by day of week. Whitelist apps that should prevent sleep.

One-Click Power Modes

Switch between Performance, Balanced, and Power Saver with one click. Each mode controls CPU, GPU, screen timeout, and power scheme.

Fullscreen & Game Detection

Automatically suspend sleep rules when a fullscreen app or game is running. Resume when you exit.

Away Mode

Detect when you step away from your PC and auto-switch to an eco-friendly power mode. Revert when you return.

Hardware Monitor

Real-time CPU, RAM, network, and FPS overlay. See exactly what your system is doing without switching apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PowerDoze free?
Yes! Core features are free forever. Pro unlocks advanced automation like app-based switching, Wi-Fi geofencing, and analytics. It's a one-time purchase, not a subscription.
Does it send my data anywhere?
No. PowerDoze runs entirely on your local machine. The only network request is an optional update check to our website. No telemetry, no analytics, no cloud.
Why does Windows SmartScreen show a warning?
Code signing certificates cost US$200+/year. As an indie developer, we'll add signing once the project is sustainable. Click "More info" then "Run anyway" to proceed safely.
Can I transfer my license to a new PC?
Yes. Use the built-in Settings Export/Import to move your configuration. License transfer will also be supported through our website.
Does it work on laptops and desktops?
Yes, both. Laptops benefit from battery policies and away mode. Desktops benefit from scheduled sleep rules and app-based power mode switching.
What happens if I don't like it?
The free version has no time limit. If you bought Pro, you have a 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.
